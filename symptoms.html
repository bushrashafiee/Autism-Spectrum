SYMPTOMS

Autism's core symptoms are social communication challenges and restricted, repetitive behavior. In autism these symptoms begin in early childhood, persist and interfere with daily living. Many people with autism have sensory issues. These typically involve over- or under-sensitivities to sounds, lights, touch, tastes, smells, pain and other stimuli. Autism is also associated with high rates of certain physical and mental health conditions. There are also signs and symptoms that we can recognized based on their growth ages.
